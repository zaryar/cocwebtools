Tip:

With the normal books from the trader, there are a few buildings that are better with a Book of Building than with a Book of Heroes:

  • Laboratory – 16 days
  • Ricochet Cannon and Multi-Archer Tower – 15 days, 12 hours
  • X-Bow, Pet House, and Air Defense – 15 days

The last 15 levels of every have the same cost of 8 days

Also:

if you want to calculate something with builder potions use: (10 * num of builders - num of builders)hours

example for 6 builders: 10*6-6=54 hours
